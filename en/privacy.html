01

Data Controller

AirFrame Media  ·  info@airframemedia.pt  ·  +351 969 442 101

02

What data do we collect?

We only collect data you provide to us directly, namely:

  • Your name and email address, when you fill in the contact form or send us a message;
  • Your phone number, if you contact us by that means;
  • Any other information you choose to share with us in the context of a request or project.

We also collect usage data in an aggregated and anonymous form, which does not allow your personal identification.

03

For what purposes do we use your data?

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your questions or quote requests;
  • Manage the contractual relationship arising from services provided;
  • Improve our website and user experience (aggregated analytics data).

We do not use your data for direct marketing purposes without your explicit consent, nor do we share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

04

Legal basis for processing

The processing of your data is based on:

  • Contract performance — when data is necessary to provide a requested service;
  • Consent — when you voluntarily contact us through the form or provide your data for specific purposes;
  • Legitimate interests — for aggregate analysis of website performance.

05

How long do we keep your data?

Data collected through the contact form is retained for as long as necessary to respond to your request and, if a contractual relationship results, for the legally required period (generally 10 years for accounting records). Data that does not lead to any contractual relationship is deleted after 12 months.

06

Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data;
  • Request the rectification of inaccurate data;
  • Request the erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten");
  • Object to processing or request its restriction;
  • Lodge a complaint with the national supervisory authority, the CNPD (www.cnpd.pt).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@airframemedia.pt.

07

Data security

The airframemedia.pt website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.), which implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, loss or destruction. For more information, see the privacy policy at github.com.

08

Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to update this policy whenever necessary. The "last updated" date at the top of this document will always reflect the most recent version.
