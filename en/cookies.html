What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device and retain certain information about your visit.

02

How do we use cookies?

The airframemedia.pt website uses strictly necessary cookies for the proper functioning of the site, as well as analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our content. We do not use advertising cookies nor share data with third parties for commercial purposes.

03

Which cookies do we use?

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for navigating the site and using its features, such as filling in forms. They do not require your consent and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies

We use analytics tools to collect aggregated and anonymous information about site usage — for example, the most visited pages and time spent. This information helps us improve the user experience. These cookies are only activated with your consent.

04

How to manage or withdraw your consent?

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through the cookie banner shown on your first visit to the site. You can also configure your browser to refuse or delete cookies, although this may affect the functioning of some site features.
